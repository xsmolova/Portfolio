SIMULATION

03. Driving Simulation

Analysis of Driver Using Eye Tracking

Brief
My master thesis was focused on the analysis of a driver behaviour when distracted by mobile communication using eye tracking. I created a driving simulation in Unity, that exposes driver to to dangerous road situations based on the information obtained from eye tracker. The aim was to compare driver's reaction time when distracted by mobile device and head-up display.

Process
Analyze domain -> Design proposal -> 3D models -> Tobii SDK -> Script for the simulation -> Critical situations -> Testing -> Analyze data

Challenges
Critical situations invoked during driving were the most important part of the case study itself. Easy extensibility of new critical situations in the future was one of the highest priorities, so I used State Machine design pattern that allows programmer to add new situations as easy as adding a new state. The transition between states was in most cases triggered by the driver's gaze. I used Tobii EyeX eyetracker for simulation.
