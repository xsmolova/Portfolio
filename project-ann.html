02. Neuroevolution

Neuroevolution for Flappy Bird game

Brief
After I took a machine learning course, I worked on the development of a neural network in C#. I recreated a popular Flappy Bird game in Unity and the behavior of each bird was controlled by a neural network. The aim was to compare the speed and efficiency of learning curve in the case of ANN, but also ANN with reinforcement learning. I further optimized these neural networks with genetic algorithm.

Process
Develop Flappy Bird game -> Artificial Neural Network -> Replace input with ANN decision -> Q-learning -> Genetic algorithm -> Tuning parameters

Challenges
The parameter tunning was the most important part of neural network optimization with a genetic algorithm. First, we tested different configurations of the neural network itself - the number of layers and the number of neurons on each layer. We combined ANN with a genetic algorithm as well as ANN with Q-learning and a genetic algorithm.
The initial problem was the insufficient exploring of the state space, so I chose a high exploration rate for the first few generations. After that the agents could score over 2045.
We also tried different selection techniques, breeding and altering mutation rate in our genetic algorithm.
